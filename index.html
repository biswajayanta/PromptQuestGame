What is CO-STAR?
CO-STAR is a simple 6-part framework for writing AI prompts that get far better results. Each letter stands for one key ingredient.
C
Context
Background the AI needs to understand your situation
O
Objective
The clear goal - what you want AI to actually do
S
Style
How the response should be written
T
Tone
The feel - professional, friendly, inspiring
A
Audience
Who will read the output
R
Response
The format - email, list, summary
